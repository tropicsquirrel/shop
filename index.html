// Specs

What's Inside the Badge

Every badge is hand-soldered and tested. No two are exactly alike — because a kid made them.

  • Raspberry Pi RP2040 (133 MHz dual-core ARM)
  • 264 KB on-chip SRAM + 2 MB Flash
  • 8× individually addressable RGB LEDs
  • USB-C port for power & programming
  • CircuitPython pre-loaded out of the box
  • 2× push buttons for badge interactions
  • I²C & UART breakout pads
  • Hidden puzzle / easter egg firmware
  • Lanyard holes + included lanyard
  • Open-source hardware & firmware (GitHub)

Hey, I'm Alex!

I'm 12 years old and I've been into electronics since I was 8. My parent took me to DEFCON for the first time last year, and I was completely blown away by the badge culture — everyone hacking, trading, and showing off their creations.

I decided to make my own badge. I learned KiCad for PCB design, hand-soldered every board myself, and wrote all the firmware in CircuitPython. It took me about four months of weekends and after-school evenings.

Every badge you buy helps me fund the next revision — and maybe a ticket back to DEFCON! Thank you for supporting a kid's dream. 🙏

// FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an official DEFCON badge?

Nope! This is a completely independent, unofficial badge created by a hobbyist kid. It is not affiliated with DEF CON Communications or the official DEFCON conference. It just happens to be awesome.

How do I program it?

Plug it in via USB-C. It shows up as a drive called CIRCUITPY. Drop a code.py file on it and your code runs instantly. No compiler, no toolchain — just Python. Full documentation is on GitHub.

What's the hidden puzzle / easter egg?

We can't give it away — that's the whole point! But we'll say: pay close attention to the LED blink pattern when you first plug it in. Morse code has been around since 1836 for a reason. 😉

How long does shipping take?

US orders ship within 2–3 business days via USPS First Class. International orders ship within 5 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

Can I get a bulk / group order?

Yes! Reach out via email for orders of 5 or more badges and we'll work something out. Great for CTF teams, school clubs, or makerspaces.

What if my badge arrives damaged?

Every badge is tested before it ships, but if something goes wrong in transit, email us with a photo and we'll send a replacement at no charge.
